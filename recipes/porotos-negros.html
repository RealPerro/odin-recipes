Porotos Negros

Description:

El que nada sabe, nada teme: Mi receta para porotos negros es una maravilla!

Ingredients

  • 500g black beans
  • One Onion
  • Two garlic cloves
  • Two Spanish Chorizos
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Rosemary
  • A can of your favorite beer

Steps

  1. Wash beans and let them soak in cold water for 24 hours
  2. Stir fry Onion, garlic and chorizo
  3. Add Beans and beer
  4. Add Salt, pepper, garlic powder and rosemary
  5. Slow cook for 2 hours
  6. Enjoy!
