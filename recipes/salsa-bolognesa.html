Home made Bolognese sauce

Description:

Actual cherry coke is crap, but you can make the most wonderful drink with coke zero and real dark cherries!

Ingredients

  • 500g Tomato paste
  • One Onion
  • Two garlic cloves
  • 250g vegetarian mince
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Rosemary

Steps

  1. Stir fry Onion, garlic and mince
  2. Add tomato sauce, 1 cup of water
  3. Add Salt, pepper, garlic powder and rosemary
  4. Slow cook for 2 hours
  5. Enjoy!
